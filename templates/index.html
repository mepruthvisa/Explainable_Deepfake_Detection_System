How it works

Neural analysis,
made transparent.

Every prediction comes with a heatmap and plain-English explanation of which part of the face gave it away.

01 · Upload
📤
Submit Any Image
Drag and drop or browse. JPEG, PNG, WebP and BMP all supported with instant validation.
02 · Detect
🧠
XceptionNet Classifies
Depthwise separable convolutions scan for GAN artifacts, texture anomalies, and unnatural blending.
03 · Explain
🔥
Grad-CAM Heatmaps
Gradient activation maps highlight exactly which pixels drove the model toward its decision.
04 · Locate
🎯
MediaPipe Landmarks
478-point face mesh maps activations to real anatomical regions — eyes, lips, cheeks, jawline.
